VIT AP University (VIT AP) is a private university that was established in 2017 and is situated in Amaravati, Andhra Pradesh. The university offers various UG, PG, and Ph.D. courses in Engineering, Management, Commerce, etc. Admission to VIT AP is done on the basis of marks obtained in the entrance examinations like VITEEE, VITMEE, VITREE, etc. The university was established as a sister institute of Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT)
